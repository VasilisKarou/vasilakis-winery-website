Men's Fragrances

Air de Corfu

Air de Corfu is a fresh, clean and utterly masculine fragrance, inspired by the coolness of the Ionian Sea. Kumquat gives a lively, sensuous burst, while subtle marine and woody notes add depth and character. A light yet powerful fragrance, ideal for the man who wants to exude freshness and confidence.

Kaiser

The Kaiser fragrance is dynamic, elegant and utterly masculine. Citrus fruits provide a bright, fresh start, while lavender adds purity and timeless freshness. Then woody and slightly spicy notes emerge, giving depth, intensity and character. A balanced and imposing fragrance, ideal for the man who exudes self-confidence and natural elegance.

Alkinoos

The Alkinoos fragrance is elegant, dynamic and sophisticatedly masculine. Bergamot gives a bright, fresh start, while lavender and geranium add aromatic purity and balance. At the base, musk and discreet spicy notes create a warm, sensual finish with depth and duration. A modern and charming fragrance, ideal for the man who wants to stand out.

Makis

The Makis fragrance is warm, sensual and full of character. Bergamot and citrus fruits open the composition with a bright, fresh note, while cinnamon adds a sweet, spicy intensity.
