The History of our Winery

In the heart of Kefalonia, in the picturesque village of Valsamata, within the Omala Valley—home to the renowned Robola wine zone—and just 800 meters from the Monastery of Saint Gerasimos, Winery Distillery Olive Oil Producer Vassilakis stands as a unique destination dedicated to the tradition of Greek wine and local products. The estate is easily accessible for buses and offers ample parking facilities.

Our winery offers visitors an authentic tasting experience, giving them the opportunity to immerse themselves in the local wine culture, sample carefully selected labels, discover traditional products of the Ionian Islands, and enjoy the warm hospitality of Kefalonia. At the same time, guests can savor our exceptional extra virgin organic olive oil, which has received awards and stands out for its quality, aroma, and distinctive flavor.
