Crystal Kumquat

A unique, crystallizing alcoholic beverage that captures the absolute purity of kumquat extract through a rare and demanding technique. Its composition is based on a fine, pure extract, which through complete boiling leads to the creation of natural crystals, giving the drink its distinctive, almost «alive» character. At its heart lies the Riki plant, which, through special processing and careful drying, is harmoniously integrated, enhancing the structure and the special character of the extract. Its uniqueness does not stop here; after consumption, with the addition of white alcohol, the natural crystals are reactivated and, within 3 to 5 days, recompose the drink in its original form, maintaining the same character and aromatic identity. An authentic creation, the result of a specialized production process, which stands out for its uniqueness and innovative character. A drink unique in conception, special in experience.

Hippophaes

A rare and refined alcoholic beverage, inspired by the precious fruit of the sea buckthorn (Hippophae rhamnoides), a symbol of strength and natural radiance since antiquity. Its name, born from the words “hippos” and “phaos”, refers to a timeless tradition of well- being and vitality. The fruit, known for its extraordinary richness in vitamins, antioxidants is rendered here, through a careful and demanding process, which highlights its purity and intensity. The result is a drink with a bright, refined character, where the natural acidity and discreet astringency are transformed into a balanced, elegant experience with depth and duration. With a strong identity and contemporary aesthetics, Hippophaes expresses a superior approach to pleasure — a meeting of nature, tradition and discreet luxury.
