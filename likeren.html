Liqueurs

Kumquat

A traditional liquor of our own production with a rich aroma and delightful taste. It is aromatic, sweet and has a full flavor with natural intensity. It is prepared from the extraction of the peel of the kumquat fruit and stands out with its bright orange color.   Other stand out characteristics are its bittersweet taste and its velvety after taste. An authentic local product of Corfu ideal as a digestive.

Lemoncello

Lemoncello is a traditional Italian liquor. The difference that makes Vassilakis’ lemoncello so unique is that it’s made by the extraction of our own local lemons. Characteristics are its bright and cloudy yellow color, intense aroma, sweet taste and a strong presence of citrus fruit. It’s ideally served with crushed ice. Best after a meal as it is a popular choice as a digestive.

Colada

Colada is a unique traditional drink prepared with pure alcohol and fresh milk following the drum method. Through this process of continuous stirring, the milk acquires a velvet texture and blends harmoniously with the other ingredients creating a balanced result. This traditional preparation technique highlights its authentic character making colada a special choice for those looking for a different and refined experience. It is available in pistachio, coffee, coconut, strawberry, banana, kumquat and lemon flavors.

Retsi

A refined alcoholic beverage that combines the natural intensity of wild cherry with the discreet aromatic structure of vermouth. Its composition captures a balanced harmony between fruit and botanical elements, creating an elegant and authentic result. The wild cherry stands out with purity, offering vibrancy and depth, while the vermouth adds a gentle, refined complexity that completes the character of the drink. With a soft texture and a fine, lingering aftertaste, Retsi expresses a quiet luxury and a modern, balanced aesthetic.
