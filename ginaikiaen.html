Women's Fragrances

Coco

Coco is warm, exotic and softly sensual. Coconut and vanilla create a sweet, summery base. Chamomile completes the composition with a calm, clean feeling. A scent that balances sweetness and freshness, leaving an elegant and soft signature.

Angie

The Angie fragrance is sensual and luxurious, with a strong feminine identity. Citrus fruits open the composition with a bright, fresh note, while jasmine and orchid provide elegance and floral softness. At the base, amber, sandalwood, patchouli, vanilla and musk create a warm, deep and magnetic imprint that lasts. A sophisticated and absolutely mysterious fragrance.

Sisi

The Sisi fragrance is romantic, bright and delicate. The rose gives a timeless femininity, while the citrus adds a fresh, lively touch. The wildflowers complete the composition with a natural, light elegance. A soft, charming fragrance that exudes freshness that awakens the senses.

Nausica

The Nausica fragrance is fresh, light and elegantly natural. Cucumber opens the composition with a clean, aquatic freshness, while fig and citrus add a soft sweetness with bright, fruity notes. Green tea gives a sense of calm and freshness, while jasmine completes the fragrance with a subtle floral elegance. A light and sophisticated fragrance, ideal for everyday freshness with natural charm.

Matina

The Matina fragrance is tender, elegant and deeply feminine. Magnolia and gardenia give a rich, floral softness, while pink rose adds romance and timeless elegance. Kumquat brightens the composition with a cool, playful note. At the base, vanilla and patchouli create a warm, sensual finish with a long-lasting effect.
