Our Sweets

Madoles/Mandolato/ Loukoumi

Mandoles: A traditional Kefalonia dessert made from roasted almonds, caramelized with sugar and flavored with honey. Crunchy, aromatic and strongly linked to local tradition.
Mandolato: A fluffy and rich dessert made from honey, sugar, egg white (meringue) and almonds. With a soft texture and balanced sweetness, traditional mandolato offers a refined taste experience.
Loukoumi: Discover the kumquat-flavored delight! Very refined combining the velvety texture of traditional delight with the aroma of exotic citrus. Each bite offers a unique balance of freshness and sweetness..
